Follow these steps to reach Tungnath:

  • After completing Kedarnath track you will return back to Sonprayag .
  • At Sonprayag you can book taxi to reach Tungnath/Chopta(Fare: 4000-6000Rs).
  • After reach Tungnath you can start your track which is 6km.
  • You can reach Tungnath via Ghoda/Khachchar(Charges: 1500-2500Rs).
  • When you reach Tungnath you can also go to track for Chandrashila which is 2km from Tungnath Temple.
  • You will see The Himalayan Peak from Chandrashila.
  • In tracking you will see natural waterfall,mountains,forests and many more intresting things.
